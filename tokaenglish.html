Hobbies

Padel

There is over 80 000 padel players these days in Finland. Its easy to start with this sport and it fits to everyone, regardless if you are an amateur or professional. Padel is a racket game where combines tennis and squash and its a doubles game.


Frisbeegolf

Frisbeegolf fits to people that loves outdoor sports. Rules are almost same as in basic golf. In frisbeegolf your goal is to throw the disc to the basket with as few throws as possible.


Athletics

Athletics is good and versatile shape of sports and you can start it whenever you want.


Swimming

If you are looking something that you can do for the rest of your life, its swimming. Swimming is one of the most popular sports in Finland and it has many good health benefits. For example it will improve your aerobic fitness, muscular endurance and maintain your muscles. Swimming is also an exellent sport for those who has sports injury for example.
